About us

Welcome to our Group Study platform "Unibuddy" — a space designed to make learning together easier and more productive. Our website brings students together with tools that encourage focus, consistency, and collaboration. With features like a study timer, habit tracker, and group study sessions, we help learners stay on track, build strong study habits, and achieve their goals. Whether you’re preparing for exams, learning new skills, or working on projects, our platform creates an environment that combines discipline with teamwork. We believe that studying doesn’t have to be a lonely journey — with the right support and tools, learning can be engaging, motivating, and fun.
